  3. Annapurna

    • The Annapurna is a portrain famous in India ,made by Nandalal Bose.
    • Nandalal Bose, a leading figure in contemporary Indian art, showcased "Annapurna" - a renowned portrayal of the goddess of nourishment to the world.
      Unlike traditional depictions of deities, Bose's Annapurna is distinguished by a kind, nurturing, and straightforward nature.
      The painting portrays the goddess with a simple and elegant style, showing her as both powerful and graceful, emphasizing her role as the divine provider. The natural colors and elegant brushwork clearly show Bose's commitment to keeping Indian art connected to its tradition while also allowing for growth.
